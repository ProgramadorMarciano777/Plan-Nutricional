### Meal Plan Option 1:

**Breakfast: Avocado Toast with French Toast Crunch Cereal**
- **Avocado Toast**: Use 1 slice of sourdough bread and half an avocado. Spread the avocado on the toasted sourdough, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
- **French Toast Crunch Cereal**: Serve 1 cup with 250ml of milk or milk alternative.

**Lunch: Angus Burger with Coleslaw**
- **Angus Burger**: Grill 1 beef angus burger patty for 5 minutes on each side. Serve in a bun with lettuce and a slice of tomato.
- **Coleslaw**: Mix 1 cup of shredded cabbage with 2 tablespoons of vegetable cream cheese for a creamy dressing.

**Dinner: Cinnamon Roll with Peanut Butter and Avocado Salad**
- **Cinnamon Roll**: Heat 1 Pillsbury Cinnamon Roll according to package instructions.
- **Peanut Butter and Avocado Salad**: Mix half an avocado with 2 tablespoons of peanut butter, serve on a bed of mixed greens.

**Snacks: M&M Cookie**
- Enjoy 1 M&M cookie.

**Total Daily Nutrition:**
- Total Calories: 2872 kcal
- Total Protein: 97g
- Total Sugar: 96g
- Total Carbohydrates: Balance with remaining calories
